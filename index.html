A little about Me

As a self-proclaimed geek and avid pc-gamer, it was only natural that I would, eventually, find my way to programming. All too often I was curious about the inner-workings of all the programs or platforms I used on a daily basis - from MMORPGs/ARPGs to Social Media to music platforms like Spotify. All of these am I interested in learning to actually build from the ground up.

As a father, son, brother, and friend, I have had no shortage of experiences with like and unlike-minded people, and, while both have taught me many lessons, I am very eager to work alongside people who have similar interests and passions as myself. Whether those people are leading and mentoring me or the reverse, I thrive in situations where the team builds off of one another. Hopefully, you and I will be able to create and debug something together in the near future.
